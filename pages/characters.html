Star Wars Characters

Discover some of the major characters that take part in the vast history of Star Wars.

Anakin Skywalker

A hero of the Clone Wars, Anakin was caring and compassionate, but suffered from a deep fear of loss that would prove to be his downfall.

Darth Vader

Once the heroic Jedi Knight named Anakin Skywalker, Darth Vader was seduced by the dark side of the Force.

Luke Skywalker

A Jedi who battled the evil Empire, discovered the truth of his parentage, and ended the tyranny of the Sith.

Ahsoka Tano

Former Jedi Knight that once served as the Padawan learner to the Jedi Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars.

Obi-Wan Kenobi

Legendary Jedi Master, a noble man and gifted in the ways of the Force. He trained Anakin Skywalker and guided Luke Skywalker as a mentor.

Padmé Amidala

A courageous and hopeful leader, serving as Queen and then Senator of Naboo.

Emperor Palpatine (Darth Sidious)

Scheming, powerful, and evil to the core, he restored the Sith and destroyed the Jedi Order. Living a double life, he was also a Naboo Senator.

Leia Organa

Princess Leia Organa was one of the greatest leaders of the Rebel Alliance, fearless on the battlefield and dedicated to ending the Empire’s tyranny.

Han Solo

Captain of the Millennium Falcon, a well known bounty hunter from Corellia that became one of the heroes of the Rebel Alliance.

Yoda

Yoda was a legendary Jedi Master and stronger than most in his connection with the Force.

Qui-Gon Jinn

A venerable if maverick Jedi Master, Qui-Gon Jinn was a student of the living Force. On Tatooine, Qui-Gon discovered a young slave boy named Anakin Skywalker who was strong in the Force.

Count Dooku

Count Dooku was a menacing Sith Lord and central figure in the Clone Wars. Once a Jedi, he became disillusioned with the Jedi Order and thirsted for greater power.

Commander Rex

Rex once served the Republic on the frontlines during the Clone Wars, taking orders from Anakin Skywalker and Ahsoka Tano as first a captain and later a commander.

C3PO and R2-D2

C-3PO, a neurotic protocol droid fluent in over six million forms of communication, and R2-D2, a resourceful astromech droid with a knack for saving the day, are an incredible duo and loyal companions and help the heroes in their adventures.

Darth Maul

A deadly, agile Sith Lord trained by the evil Darth Sidious, Darth Maul was a formidable warrior and scheming mastermind.
