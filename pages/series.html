Star Wars Series

The Star Wars franchise has spawned multiple television series, the first being the animated series Clone Wars, which focused on the prequel trilogy-era. There has been numerous tv shows released since then, bringing some of the most epic stories to animated and live-action format as well.

Animated Series

The Clone Wars

2008

The series sees Anakin Skywalker, Obi-Wan Kenobi, Yoda, Ahsoka Tano and other characters as they struggle against the dark side during the divisive, action-packed Clone Wars.

Rebels

2014

Star Wars Rebels, set five years before the events of Star Wars: A New Hope, chronicles the beginnings of the Rebellion as the Empire spreads tyranny across the galaxy.

The Bad Batch

2021

The clone special unit called "The Bad Batch", make their way through the newly founded empire as they try to find out why all clone troops, except themselves, opposed their Jedi generals.

Visions

2021

This anthology of animated shorts from around the world celebrates the Star Wars mythos across different cultures.

Tales of the Jedi

2022

Delve into the lives of two very different Jedi: Ahsoka Tano and Count Dooku. Each of them will be put to the test as they make decisions that will define their destiny.

Live-Action Shows

The Mandalorian

2019

After the fall of the Empire, a lone Mandalorian makes his way through the lawless galaxy with his adopted son, Grogu.

The Book of Boba Fett

2021

In the sands of Tatooine, bounty hunter Boba Fett and mercenary Fennec Shand navigate the underworld of the Galaxy and fight for Jabba the Hutt's former territory.

Obi-Wan Kenobi

2022

During the reign of the Empire, Obi-Wan Kenobi embarks on a crucial mission.

Andor

2022

In a dangerous time, Cassian Andor embarks on a path destined to make him a rebel hero.

Ahsoka

2023

Set after the fall of the Empire, "Ahsoka" follows former Jedi knight Ahsoka Tano as she investigates an emerging threat to a vulnerable galaxy.
